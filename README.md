# TELUS Rewards Content Dashboard

A centralized content management hub for creating, scheduling, and managing TELUS Rewards program marketing content across all channels.

## Features

✨ **AI Content Generator** - Generate brand-aligned content using Claude AI
- Creates content for newsletter, social media, email, and more
- Automatically follows TELUS Rewards brand guidelines
- Incorporates selected catalogue items into content

📅 **Calendar View** - See all upcoming content at a glance
- Monthly calendar showing scheduled content
- Color-coded by channel for easy tracking
- Quick navigation between months

📋 **Content Management** - Organize and track all content
- Create, edit, and delete content pieces
- Track status: Draft → Pending Review → Scheduled → Published
- View all content in a clean list format

🔄 **Content Repurposing** - Adapt content for multiple channels
- Take newsletter content and automatically create social media versions
- Generate Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, and email variations
- AI optimizes each version for platform best practices

📚 **Full Catalogue** - Browse all available offers and perks
- My TELUS Perks (roaming passes, counselling, etc.)
- My Partner Perks (WestJet, Skip, Turo, Hotel Credits, etc.)
- Gift Cards (Amazon, Uber, DoorDash, Spotify, iTunes)
- Contests

📖 **Brand Guidelines** - Quick reference for messaging standards
- Key messaging pillars
- Brand voice guidelines
- Correct terminology and what to avoid
- Tier names and metrics

## How to Use

### Creating Content

1. Click **"New Content"**
2. Fill in:
   - **Title** - Name your content piece
   - **Channel** - Select where it will be published
   - **Date** - When you want it published
   - **Content** - Write or generate the copy
   - **Featured Items** (optional) - Select catalogue items to highlight

3. Either:
   - **Write manually** and click "Save"
   - **Use AI** - Click "AI Generate", describe what you want, and Claude creates it

### Repurposing Content

1. Go to **Content** view
2. Find the content you want to repurpose (e.g., a newsletter)
3. Click the ⚡ (lightning bolt) icon
4. Select which channels to adapt for (Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, Email)
5. Click "Repurpose" - Claude automatically creates optimized versions for each platform

### Tracking Status

Content flows through these stages:
- 👁️ **Draft** → Click eye icon to mark "Pending Review"
- ✅ **Pending** → Click check icon to "Approve & Schedule"
- 📅 **Scheduled** → Ready to publish
- ✨ **Published** → Live to members

### Viewing Your Schedule

1. Click **"Calendar"** to see all content by date
2. Use the navigation buttons to move between months
3. Hover over content pieces to see titles

### Browse the Catalogue

1. Click **"Catalogue"** 
2. View all available:
   - TELUS Perks (with tier requirements)
   - Partner Perks
   - Gift Cards (with point costs)
   - Contests

Select any of these when creating content so Claude knows to feature them.

### Brand Guidelines

Click **"Brand Guide"** to reference:
- TELUS Rewards positioning statement
- Key messaging pillars (Inclusive, Simple, Meaningful, Rewarding)
- Brand voice (Customers First, Innovative, Engaging)
- What to always use (members, TELUS Rewards, earn points)
- What to never say (customers, free perks, best program)
- Tier names and metrics

## Important Brand Rules

✅ **Always Use:**
- "TELUS Rewards" (not Telus)
- "members" (not customers)
- "earn points" and "redeem points"
- Capitalized tier names: Purple, Gold, Platinum, Diamond
- "Zero-point contest" (not "free contest")

❌ **Never Use:**
- "Telus Rewards"
- "customers" 
- "free perks"
- "best loyalty program in Canada"
- Tier names as adjectives (say "Platinum tier benefits" not "Platinum benefits")

## Data Storage

- All content is saved in your browser's local storage
- Your data persists between sessions
- Catalogue items are built-in and always available
- No data is sent to external servers except to Claude AI for content generation

## AI Content Generation

The dashboard uses Claude AI (Anthropic) to generate content. When you click "AI Generate":

1. Your description is sent to Claude
2. Claude receives your brand guidelines
3. Claude receives your selected catalogue items
4. Claude generates brand-compliant content
5. The content appears in your form for editing before saving

## Team Access

Share this dashboard URL with your team. Everyone who accesses it will:
- See and manage the same content calendar
- Have access to the brand guidelines
- Be able to generate new content
- Track content status across channels

## Tips for Best Results

- **Be specific in your descriptions** - "Summer roaming pass promotion for Instagram" works better than "summer promo"
- **Select catalogue items** before generating - Claude will naturally incorporate them
- **Use repurposing** to save time - One newsletter becomes multiple social posts
- **Check brand guide** before publishing - Ensure terminology is correct
- **Iterate on AI content** - Generate, edit, refine, then save

## Support

For issues or questions:
- Check the Brand Guide for messaging rules
- Review the Catalogue to see what's available
- Regenerate content if the first version needs improvement
- Try more specific descriptions when generating
